# Bigbasket Brand Store Creative Guidelines

## 1. NPC (New Product Carousel) Guidelines
**Purpose:** Used to highlight categories, brand-related products, sponsored brands, and seasonal festivities.
**General Structure:** Horizontal carousel with a fixed "Offer callout" at the top to maintain uniformity while scrolling.

### Template Types & Dimensions
*Note: All sizes mentioned in anatomy diagrams are reference sizes. Final designs must be uploaded at **3x** scale.*

#### **Medium Template**
*   **Total Dimensions:** 360px (W) x 480px (H)
*   **Corner Radius:** 12px
*   **Image Placeholder:** 330px x 330px (Centered horizontally)
    *   *Effect:* Drop Shadow (Blur: 16, Color: #000000, Opacity: 40%)
*   **Logo Area:** 360px (W) x 102px (H)
    *   *Style:* Bottom aligned, background opacity 50%, background blur 40.
    *   *Stroke:* Linear gradient (White 0% to 100% to 0%).
*   **Offer Callout (Fixed Top):** 51px (W) x 252px (H)
    *   *Background:* Black (#000000), Opacity 40%.
*   **Typography:**
    *   *Title:* Font: Rubik Medium | Color: #2B2B2B or #FFFFFF | Size: 10px (Line height 133%).
    *   *Offer Text:* Font: Rubik Medium | Color: #FFFFFF | Size: 9px (Line height 133%).

#### **Small Template**
*   **Total Dimensions:** 288px (W) x 384px (H)
*   **Corner Radius:** 8px
*   **Image Placeholder:** 285px x 285px
    *   *Effect:* Drop Shadow (Blur: 16, Color: #000000, Opacity: 40%)
*   **Logo Area:** 360px (W) x 90px (H)
*   **Offer Callout:** 51px (W) x 249px (H)

### Design Rules
*   **Background:** Depends on Brand Color.
*   **Variations:** Templates can exist **With** or **Without** the "Offer Callout."
*   **Elements Checklist:**
    *   Offer Callout (Optional)
    *   Title (Optional/Mandatory based on layout)
    *   Product Image (Mandatory)
    *   Logo (Mandatory)

## 2. Banner ATC (Add To Cart) Widget
**Purpose:** Highlight brands, support static images, and show a range of products from a particular brand via a horizontal carousel.

### Anatomy & Dimensions
*   **Full Widget Size:** 1080px (W) x 1126px (H)
*   **Header Banner Area:** 1080px (W) x 312px (H)
*   **Logo Specification:**
    *   Position: Fixed on the left.
    *   Dimensions: 48px (W) x 36px (H) (Ratio 4:3).

### Creative Guidelines
1.  **Product Image Alignment:** Always align product images to the **right**.
2.  **Background:** Background imagery can extend full width behind the product.
3.  **Headings:**
    *   Product heading will take **2 lines** if longer.
    *   "View more" link placed below the SKUs.
4.  **Spacing:**
    *   Padding of **12px** is standard around the product cards within the widget.

---

## 3. Grid (Brand Store) Guidelines
**Purpose:** Used for highlighting brands, grouping same-category brands, or sponsored brands. Displayed in multiple rows.

### Anatomy
*   **Cell Aspect Ratio:** 1:1 (Square)
*   **Cell Dimensions:** 225px (W) x 225px (H)
*   **Background Colour:** Customizable based on brand (e.g., #FFEDE6).

### Spacing Rules
*   **Horizontal Padding:** 16px (Outer edges).
*   **Vertical Padding:** 12px (Between Brand Header and Cells).
*   **Internal Gutter:** 12px (Between individual cells).

---

## 4. Spotlight Guidelines
**Purpose:** High-impact area supporting video or static images followed by a product carousel.

### Anatomy
*   **Total Widget Size:** 1080px (W) x 882px (H)
*   **Media Container (Video/Image):**
    *   Dimensions: 1080px (W) x 540px (H).
    *   Aspect Ratio: **3:2**.
    *   *Design Note:* Defined height ensures content isn't cut off from the bottom while scrolling.
*   **SKU Gradient Overlay:**
    *   Dimensions: 360px (W) x 160px (H).
    *   Style: Linear Gradient (Top to Bottom), Color #000000, Opacity 0% to 100%.

### Design Rules
1.  **Logo Placement:** **Avoid** placing logos on the **Top Left** of the video/image (reserved for "View All" CTA).
2.  **SKU Alignment:** SKUs are aligned to the left.
3.  **Card Sizing:** The first card in the carousel is always displayed bigger than the subsequent cards.

---

## 5. General Banner Guidelines
**Typography:** All banners utilize the **Rubik** font family.

### A. Regular Templates
**Structure:** Split layout.
*   **Content (Left):** 55% width.
*   **Imagery (Right):** 45% width.

**Text Rules:**
*   **Title:** Maximum of **2 lines**.
*   **Supporting Text / Offer Callout:** Maximum of **2 lines**.

**Required Elements (Template dependent):**
*   Logo, Title, CTA Button.
*   *Optional:* Special Text (e.g., "Hot Deals"), Offer Tag, Subtext.

### B. Strip Templates
**Structure:** Slim horizontal layout.
**Constraints:**
*   **Product Images:** Do not use more than **3 product images**.

**Variations:**
*   With Offer Callout (Right arrow CTA).
*   "Powered By" Label.
